Human NonVerbal Language Dataset



The goal of Human NonVerbal Language Dataset (HumanNoVeLa ) is to gather data related to human body patterns that can hopefully be used to study nonverbal communication characteristics.

A context in which nonverbal information play a major role is the financing of young firms. We videotaped 218 entrepreneurs in a real investment setting, and surveyed independent raters to obtain perceptions of each speaker on six individual characteristics: passion, dominance, trustworthiness, competence, attractiveness and openness (those that prior literature examines in relation to financing contexts).

HumanNoVeLa offers speaker's 3D skeleton videos extracted from those videos and their respective ratings on perceptions of speakers' personal characteristics.

What can be found?
* Entrepreneurs dataset: contains data from 218 entrepreneurial pitching sessions in the form of 3D body pose estimation and nonverbal characteristics.
* Feel free to contact us if you want to add extra datasets! :)
